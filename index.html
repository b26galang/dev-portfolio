About Me

Growing up with a brother and father who shared a love for video games, I naturally gravitated towards the same hobbies. My childhood memories of video games began with the Halo series on Xbox. My interest in video games eventually led me to web development, where I'm constantly exploring new technologies and languages to bring my visions to life.

One of my proudest achievements is embracing back-end development, despite my initial hesitation towards number-oriented work. While I initially envisioned myself in a UI developer or graphic designer role, I've since embraced the versatility of different developer types and am open to new opportunities.

I firmly believe in taking pride in one's work, embracing challenges, and constantly pushing boundaries. Whether I'm exploring open-world adventures to aim training in competitive first person shooter games, I'm captivated by the ever-evolving world of video games and the stunning graphics they offer.

In my pursuit for knowledge, I've ventured into various learning platforms, including mobile apps, to diversify my learning environment and keep my routine fresh. My favorite for learning C# has been Sololearn!
